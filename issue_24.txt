**Context**
The system is currently unsafe (no low-battery shutdown) and the logout menu is unstyled (bare text). We need a safety daemon and a visual overhaul for the power menu.
*(Note: Waybar cleanup is handled in #22)*

**Instructions**
Please generate the code/scripts to perform the following:

**1. Battery Safety (`batsignal`)**
* **Install:** `batsignal`.
* **Hyprland Config:** Add `exec-once = batsignal -w 15 -c 5 -d 2 -p` to `hyprland.conf`.
    * Warn at 15%, Critical at 5%, Suspend at 2%.

**2. Visual Power Menu (`wlogout`)**
* **Install:** `wlogout`.
* **Layout (`layout`):** Configure standard buttons: `lock`, `logout`, `suspend`, `hibernate`, `reboot`, `shutdown`.
* **Style (`style.css`):**
    * **Theme:** Match Catppuccin Mocha.
    * **Background:** Semi-transparent black overlay.
    * **Buttons:** Use CSS `background-image: image(url("..."))` to load icons instead of text labels.
    * **Hover:** Add a simple hover effect (lighten background).

**Definition of Done:**
* Laptop notifies and beeps at 15% battery.
* Clicking the power button opens a graphical icon grid.
